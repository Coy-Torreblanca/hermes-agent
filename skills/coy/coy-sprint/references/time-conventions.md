# Time Conventions Reference

Captured from Coy's explicit corrections during sessions.

## "Tomorrow" at Midnight

When Coy says "tomorrow" at 12 AM (midnight), he means the **same calendar day** after sleeping — NOT the following calendar day.

```
Example: 12 AM Sunday → "tomorrow" = Sunday (not Monday)
Example: 12 AM Saturday → "tomorrow" = Saturday (not Sunday)
```

This caused a real bug: reminders were shifted a day forward incorrectly.

Coy's daily work cadence:
```
2hr focus → 15min break → 2hr focus → 30min break → 2hr focus → 15min break
```

## Sabbath Boundary

When Coy says "it's my sabbath" or "I need to be thinking on God":
- He's clearing his mind, not being productive
- Save what he dictates silently
- No next steps, no "want me to…", no task proposals
- Quiet, minimal tone
